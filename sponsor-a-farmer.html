Project Details

Sponsor-a-Farmer

  • We help local farmers and fisherfolks by giving them grocery packs, term life insurance, hermetic technology bags which they can use to improve the shelf life of their product and water-bill subsidy for a month.
  • By donating Php 500.00 or 11.00 USD, you can already sponsor a farmer and his/her family to receive grocery packs, term life insurance, hermetic technology bags and water-bill subsidy.

Process of selecting farmer benefeciaries:

  • Our team will communicate with the Mayor's office or the local Agriculturist to get the list of farmers in different barangays.
  • We also communicate with the farmer leader to let them know how many farmers we are going to give per barangay.
  • Once list of beneficiaries has been finalized we will let the farmer leader know the schedule of the dispatch.

They will receive:

  • Grocery Packs (each grocery packs costs Php500-Php550)
  • Free 1-year term life insurance (partner: Country Bankers Insurance Group or CBIG)
  • One-month bill subsidy (Waves4Water)
  • Hermetic technology bags (5 bags each farmer)
