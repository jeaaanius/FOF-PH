PROJECT SAGIP FINANCIAL SUBSIDY

In partnership with For the Future PH and Kids for Kids. an initiative to help and support local farmers severely affected by the recent typhoons.

How can you help:

  • Donate 2,000 (or 42 USD) to sponsor a farmer
  • Donate any amount, will be included in the pool of funds of Project Sagip

Majority of our farmers depend heavily on loans for their capital during the planting season. Because of the recent typhoons, their crops were washed out and were damaged, leaving our farmers with negative profit.
